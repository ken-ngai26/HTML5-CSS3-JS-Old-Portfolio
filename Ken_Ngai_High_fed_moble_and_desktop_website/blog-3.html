Hastings Park Greenway Loop by Cycling in Vancouver

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. Hastings Park Greenway Loop

Posted on June 24, 2017 at 9:00 PM

« »

This route is an 11 km long cycling loop, it includes a bit of downhill and uphill climbing. So it will be hard on the leg for beginners, besides doing the full loop you can stop by and relax or do other stuff in the Hastings Park. There are lots to see and do in Hastings Park other than PNE or Playland.

  • Road: All paved
  • Difficulty level: Intermediate
  • Safety level: Safe for the whole family
  • Suitable for: Everyone

Gardens and open space

You can bring your families with you and gather around the large feast table at the Italian Garden for a family picnic. Some can go take the bike and do the Hastings Park Greenway Loop. While some can go relax beside The Sanctuary Pond or the Momiji Gardens and enjoy the nature.

Empire Fields and Plateau Sports Park

After a picnic, you can head over to the Empire Fields and the Plateau Sports Park to get some exercise done. Here is a list some of the activities you can do here:

  • Basketball
  • Beach Volleyball
  • Parkour
  • Outdoor fitness equipment
  • Table tennis
  • Playground
  • Bike skills park
  • Play soccer on the fields and run the track at Empire Fields

It is a great way to spend the day for kids and families of all ages.
