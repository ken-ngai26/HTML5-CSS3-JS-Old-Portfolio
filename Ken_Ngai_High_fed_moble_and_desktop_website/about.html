About Cycling In Vancouver


What is Cycling In Vancouver?

Cycling In Vancouver is a non-profit organization that provides platform and guides to enhance the cycling experience for cyclists in Vancouver. It is a Metro Vancouver route planner, which lets you plan different kind of cycling routes from A to B by difficulties. It is designed by cyclist community, for the cyclist community, and give a easier start to the novice cyclist in Vancouver. New cyclist can join the community by signing up and able to join group cycling formed by the community’s cyclist. It can also be used by tourist cyclist who enjoy finding new cycling route, cycling together and make new friends.

Our Goal

We want people of all ages, and abilities to be able to cycle enjoyably, safely, and easily.

Our Core Values

  • Respect all forms of transportation
  • Value cycling as a community building tool
  • Vancouver being the greenest city in the world

Origins

Cycling In Vancouver was founded from making Vancouver the greenest city, and a belief that cycling experience are always better when shared with community. We can share all kind of bike rides experience, the good and the bad. As our community grow, we will achieve making Vancouver the greenest city.

Our Team

Ken Ngai
President

Ken believes that cycling creates cleaner and safer communities. He would like cycling to be fully integrated into Vancouver transportation network along with buses, motorcycles, cars and pedestrians. We are all part of this network and cycling should be made safer for both cyclists and non-cyclists.
