Planning your route?

By using our route planner, it will be very easy to plan your cycling route. Just by entering your starting point and ending point, it will calculate a route by shortest distance or safest depending on your choice.

How do I print or email the route that I inserted?

By using our route planner, it will be very easy to plan your cycling route. Just by entering your starting point and ending point, it will calculate a route by shortest distance or safest depending on your choice.

How do I rate the route?

After calculating the route, there will be direct instruction of how to cycle to the destination. Scroll down to the bottom and there will be a link to Rate route, Choose one of the rating and give any feedback on the route.

What is rating?

Ratings show star ratings on the pre-planned route, which was posted by a cyclist in the community and wrote a guide on it. Then the route can be rated by the community on the planner after testing the route by themselves. These ratings help others cyclist with their decisions on their next cycling route.

How do I sign up?

  1. Click on the Signup page on the top right corner of the site.
  2. Fill in the form.
  3. Verification will be sent to your email.
  4. Verify it by confirming it on the email and you are done.

How do I log in?

  1. Sign up an account.
  2. From navigation bar, go to Login.
  3. On the Login page, enter your username and password.
  4. Click on the Login button.

How do I create a group cycling?

  1. Sign up an account.
  2. Log in to the account.
  3. From navigation bar, go to group cycling and click on Create.
  4. on the Create page, fill in the form such as date, location, description and contact info.

How do I join the group cycling?

  1. Sign up an account.
  2. Log in to the account.
  3. From navigation bar, go to group cycling and click on Create.
  4. On the Join page, choose one of the groups you wanted to join and click on join below the post.
