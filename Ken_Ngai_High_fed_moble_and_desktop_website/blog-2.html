False Creek Seawall Loop by Cycling in Vancouver

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. False Creek Seawall Loop

Posted on June 24, 2017 at 9:00 PM

« »

Sometimes, the best way to see downtown Vancouver, is to appreciate it from a distance. The False Creek loop allows just that, it is a 9.11 km ride as you cycle along the south shores of the narrow water separating the rest of Vancouver from its core.

  • Road: All paved
  • Difficulty level: Easy
  • Safety level: Safe for the whole family
  • Suitable for: Everyone

Follow the map below and check out some of the sightseeing point!


Sightseeing Point

  • Yaletown
  • BC Place Stadium

  • Cambie Bridge
  • Granville Bridge
  • Granville Island
  • Burrard Bridge

The beautiful urban view across the water is what makes this route one of the best in Vancouver.
